2. Data Preprocessing

Segmenting large text files

If you have large text files (e.g. novels), it is recommended to segment the whole text into smaller parts, e.g. 1000 characters per segment. Type a number into the following field. If you don't want to segment your files, leave the field blank.

Tokenizing text into words

Regular expressions are one more or less reliable way to split a text into words. You can tokenize your text files with an integrated (and proved) regular expression for English, German, French, Spanish and Portuguese. If you want to submit your own regular expression, type it in the following text field.

Stopwords and Hapax Legomena

You can determine stopwords and hapax legomena individually based on your corpus or upload your own words-to-remove-list.
